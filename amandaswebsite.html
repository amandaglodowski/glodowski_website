AMANDA GLODOWSKI



A fixture of NYC, Amanda has called 3 of 5 boroughs home. She spent her collegiate summers working at her hometown paper, The Brooklyn Reporter, where she covered everything from politics to garbage pickups. She took a short hiatus from city life to study English rhetoric and economic policy analysis at Binghamton University. After graduation she worked for nearly four years as a research and analytics consultant for clients ranging from Fortune 500 companies to small, mission driven organizations. As a journalist, Amanda strives to leverage her expertise in data-driven narratives to tell stories through numbers.

She is working on being more active on Twitter, but for now you can check out her adventures on Instagram.
